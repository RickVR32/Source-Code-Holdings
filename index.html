01 — The Framework

A law, not
a theory

The Source Code Framework was reverse-engineered from lived human experience — not built top-down from academic theory. An original, internally consistent model of human awareness. The language, structure, and all terminology are proprietary intellectual property of Source Code Holdings Ltd.

"Everything in existence must coincide with its polar opposite or cease to exist."
This is the single principle from which the entire framework derives. Not a metaphor — a structural rule. It determines why the framework is shaped the way it is, why its measurement system works the way it does, and why its developmental model follows a spiral rather than a line.

The practical implication: no code, state, or pattern should be eliminated. The goal is never to remove the negative — it is to balance all components so that none dominates to the point of dysfunction.

02 — Observer Codes

Not types.
Percentages.

The framework's most original contribution. Four Observer Codes present in every person simultaneously, always summing to 100%. The ratio between them determines current state and predicts behavioural output. Zero-Point — fully balanced awareness — is 25% across all four simultaneously.

NARC

The negative percentage

Extractive, self-protective, survival-oriented energy. Not inherently harmful — at 25% it provides necessary self-preservation. When dominant it becomes zero-sum. Where every human awareness begins.

  • Polar pair: YARC
  • At 25%: healthy self-interest
  • When dominant: manipulative, zero-sum
YARC

The positive percentage

Giving, other-directed energy. The polar opposite of NARC. At 25% it produces empathy and genuine connection. When dominant, self-abandoning. Not widely recognised as a dysfunction — society rewards self-sacrifice.

  • Polar pair: NARC
  • At 25%: empathy, connection
  • When dominant: self-abandoning
CIST

The balanced percentage

The integration code. Holds NARC and YARC in conscious regulation — does not eliminate either, contains both. When dominant, the most stable self-aware functioning state available below Zero-Point.

  • Polar pair: Øll
  • At 25%: stable, grounded
  • When dominant: rigid, over-analytical
Øll

The all-and-nothing percentage

Pure potential in both directions simultaneously. At 25% it provides creative intensity and transformational energy. When dominant, total volatility — behaviour that cannot be predicted even by the person themselves.

  • Polar pair: CIST
  • At 25%: creative intensity
  • When dominant: implosion or explosion

04 — Development

A spiral,
not a line

Human awareness follows a natural sequence governed by the polar opposite law. Each cycle completes and begins again at a higher level of awareness. The sequence is fixed. The depth of each pass through it changes.

Stage 01 → 02

Origin

Øll first — pure potential, no formed identity. Then NARC: self-reference. I exist. I need. I feel. Where every human awareness begins. Not a flaw — a structural necessity governed by the foundational law.

  • Øll → NARC sequence
  • Pre-conscious to self-aware
  • The necessary starting point
Stage 03 → 04

Integration

NARC learning duality — right and wrong, cause and effect, consequence. Becoming CIST: regulation, holding self and other in balance simultaneously. The stable functional adult state.

  • NARC → CIST transition
  • Duality and consequence
  • Stable self-aware functioning
Stage 05 → 06

Cycle completion

CIST → YARC: genuine other-awareness without losing self. Then Øll again — the known meets the unknown. The spiral restarts at a higher level. Each cycle produces more conscious awareness than the last.

  • CIST → YARC → Øll
  • The spiral restarts higher
  • Growth is never linear
