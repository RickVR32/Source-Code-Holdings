02 — Observer Codes

Not types. Percentages.

Four Observer Codes are present in every human system simultaneously, always summing to 100%. The ratio between them determines current state and predicts behavioural output. Zero-Point — fully balanced awareness — is 25% across all four simultaneously. The framework's most original contribution to the field.

NARC

The negative percentage

Extractive, self-protective, survival-oriented energy. Not inherently harmful — at 25% it provides necessary self-preservation. When dominant it becomes zero-sum. The starting point of all human awareness.

Polar pair: YARC · At 25%: healthy self-interest · When dominant: manipulative, zero-sum

YARC

The positive percentage

Giving, other-directed energy. The polar opposite of NARC. At 25% it produces empathy and genuine connection. When dominant, self-abandoning — a dysfunction not widely recognised as such.

Polar pair: NARC · At 25%: empathy, connection · When dominant: self-abandoning

CIST

The balanced percentage

The integration code. Holds NARC and YARC in conscious regulation — does not eliminate either, contains both. When dominant, the most stable self-aware functioning state available below Zero-Point.

Polar pair: Øll · At 25%: stable, grounded · When dominant: rigid, over-analytical

Øll

The all-and-nothing percentage

Pure potential in both directions simultaneously. At 25% it provides creative intensity and transformational energy. When dominant, total volatility — behaviour that cannot be predicted even by the system itself.

Polar pair: CIST · At 25%: creative intensity · When dominant: implosion or explosion

Everything = Quadrality, or Nothing — dependent on the Observer.
